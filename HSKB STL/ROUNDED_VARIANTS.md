Notes:
- Switch/stabilizer cutouts and all mating surfaces are untouched — switch fit and case assembly are preserved.

- A 2.5 mm round needs **no supports**; print as you would the originals.
- Suggested material: **silk PLA** (matches the original look) or **PETG** (tougher). Generated with Blender.
